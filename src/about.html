About Mealner

Mealner is a meal finder application that helps users discover, plan, and manage meal with ease. Powered by TheMealDB API, users can search by meal name or category and view details, including ingredients, preparation instructions, and even embedded cooking tutorials. Whether planning meals for a day or looking for a specific recipe, Mealner simplifies the process, offering a convenient and user-friendly experience for food enthusiasts and home cooks.
